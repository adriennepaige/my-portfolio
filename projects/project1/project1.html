Super Mario Zine

A handmade zine exploring visual storytelling through collage, typography, and playful references to classic Nintendo imagery.

Editorial Design 2024 Adobe InDesign

Concept

This project explored how nostalgic imagery and playful typography could be combined into a short-form publication. The goal was to capture the visual energy of early video game graphics while maintaining a clear editorial structure.

Poster

Promotional poster created to accompany the publication. The design highlights the bold primary color palette and oversized type inspired by early Nintendo graphics.
