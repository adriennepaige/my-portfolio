About Me

Hi, I’m Adrienne.

I’m a graphic design student focused on branding, editorial design, and web design. I love work that feels visually bold, expressive, and thoughtfully put together, especially when it combines strong typography, color, and a little bit of experimentation.

A lot of my ideas start with collecting inspiration and building a mood first. I’m especially drawn to collage, layered imagery, print-based projects, and hand-coded websites that still feel personal and human.

branding editorial web design typography collage creative coding

designer, mom, and professional overthinker

What I care about

I care about making work that feels clear, intentional, and full of personality. I like design that communicates well but still has texture, edge, and emotion. Whether I’m working on a publication, a brand system, or a website, I want the final piece to feel cohesive and memorable.

How I work

My process usually starts with visual research, collecting references, and figuring out the mood first. From there, I build systems through typography, layout, image treatment, and color.

Outside of design

I’m also a mom, and that balance between creativity and everyday life shapes the way I work and think as a designer. It keeps me grounded, resourceful, and very aware of how design fits into real life.

Right now

Right now I’m interested in building a portfolio that shows range across branding, editorial, and web while still feeling like me.
